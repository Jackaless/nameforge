About this name generator

Fantasy & D&D names

Our fantasy name generator draws on naming conventions from high fantasy literature and tabletop RPG traditions. Elven names are designed to feel melodic and ancient, dwarven names carry their characteristic strength and brevity, and fae names reflect the liminal, natural quality of that tradition.

D&D players will find class-specific name suggestions — a Wizard's name has different phonetic qualities than a Fighter's. Tone and archetype are baked in to help you find a name that fits your character concept, not just the setting.

Baby names by origin

Each baby name in our database includes its meaning — because the most memorable names carry a story. We cover Celtic, Scandinavian, Latin, Greek, Hebrew, Japanese and Arabic origins, reflecting the rich diversity of naming traditions parents draw on today.

Unlike baby name lists that surface the same hundred popular names, we've curated options that are distinctive without being invented. Classic but not overused.

Business & brand names

A good business name is short, distinctive, and available. Our business name generator offers five distinct style registers — from clean modern coinages like "Velo" and "Nuvola" to professional partnership-style names. Use these as inspiration, then check domain availability for your favourites.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these names commercially?

Yes. All names generated by this tool are free to use for any purpose — personal projects, published fiction, games, business names, and more. Names themselves are not subject to copyright. If a generated name happens to match an existing trademark, check clearance before commercial use.

How are the names generated?

Names are drawn from hand-curated lists built around each category's conventions — phonetic patterns for fantasy races, etymological research for baby names, branding principles for business names. The randomisation happens entirely in your browser; no names are sent to or stored on any server.

Can I suggest names to add?

Yes — use the contact link in the footer. We regularly update each category's name pool based on feedback, particularly for underrepresented cultural origins in the baby names section.

How many names can I generate?

Unlimited. Click Generate as many times as you like. Each click draws fresh random selections from the curated pool for your chosen category and filters.
